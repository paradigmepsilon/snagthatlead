Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

SnagThatLead AI ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website snagthatlead.com and provides AI-powered lead response services to local service businesses. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our website, services, or communicate with us.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and business information when you sign up for our service or request a lead response audit
  • Payment information processed through Stripe (we do not store credit card numbers on our servers)
  • Messages you send through our chat widget or via SMS

Information collected from your customers (on your behalf):

  • Phone numbers of individuals who call your business phone number and are routed through our system
  • SMS message content between your customers and our AI assistant
  • Chat messages sent through the website chat widget installed on your site
  • Appointment booking details

Information collected automatically:

  • IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring URLs
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies for website analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide our AI lead response service, including chat, SMS, and missed-call text-back features
  • To process payments and manage your account
  • To communicate with you about your account, service updates, and support
  • To improve our services, including AI response quality and system performance
  • To generate performance reports and analytics for your business
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. SMS and Messaging

Our service sends SMS messages to your customers on your behalf when they call your business and the call is not answered. By using our service, you confirm that you have the right to communicate with your customers via SMS for business purposes.

Your customers can opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. Replying HELP to any message will return a short help response with contact information. We honor all opt-out requests immediately.

Typical message frequency is 1 missed-call text-back, any replies the customer sends as part of the same conversation, and 1 appointment confirmation text if a booking is made. Message and data rates may apply.

Phone numbers and SMS opt-in data are not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes. No mobile information will be sold.

For full details on our messaging practices, see our SMS Terms and Conditions.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our platform:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): Powers our conversational AI for chat and SMS responses. Messages are processed by Anthropic's API. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Twilio: Handles phone calls, SMS messaging, and phone number provisioning. See Twilio's Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe: Processes payments. We do not store credit card information. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel: Hosts our application infrastructure. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Chat and SMS conversation data is retained for up to 90 days for service quality and support purposes, unless you request earlier deletion.

If you cancel your account, we will delete your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL), secure API authentication, and access controls. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@snagthatlead.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
