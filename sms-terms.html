Messaging Flow Diagram

This diagram shows every scenario in which a text message is sent through our platform.

📞

Customer Action

Customer calls the business phone number

The customer initiates contact by calling the business. This is the only trigger for the SMS flow. No messages are sent without the customer calling first.

🔔

System

Call rings for 20 seconds, forwarded to business owner

If answered: Normal phone call. No text messages are sent. Flow ends here.
If not answered: Voicemail plays. System detects the missed call and proceeds to Step 3.
💬

Automated (1 message)

Missed-call text-back sent to customer

Within 60 seconds of the missed call, the system sends a single text message to the customer's phone number.

"Hi, thanks for calling Smith's Plumbing! Sorry we missed you. How can we help?"
📝

Customer Action

Customer replies (or doesn't)

No reply: No further messages sent. Flow ends here. The customer received only 1 text.
Customer replies: Example: "I have a leaking pipe in my kitchen." Conversation continues to Step 5.
🤖

AI-Assisted Response

AI assistant responds with qualifying questions

The AI assistant, trained on the business's services and information, responds to help the customer. One question at a time. Responses are kept under 160 characters.

"We can help with that. Is the leak active right now, or is it a slow drip? And are you in the Douglasville area?"
📅

AI-Assisted Response

Appointment booking (when customer is ready)

Once the customer's needs are understood, the AI assistant offers to book an appointment and provides a booking link.

"Got it. We can get someone out this week. Book a time that works for you here: https://cal.com/smiths-plumbing"
✅

System

Appointment confirmation

After the customer books, a single confirmation message is sent.

"Your appointment is confirmed for Thursday at 2pm. If anything changes, just reply to this text."
⛔

Opt-Out (available at any step)

Customer replies STOP at any time

If the customer replies STOP, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, or QUIT at any point in the conversation, all messaging stops immediately. A single confirmation is sent.

"You have been unsubscribed. No further messages will be sent. Reply START if you change your mind."

1. Program Description

SnagThatLead AI provides automated text messaging services on behalf of local service businesses (such as plumbers, HVAC companies, dentists, electricians, and other service providers). When you call a participating business and the call is not answered, our system sends you a text message so the business can assist you as quickly as possible.

Our messaging program includes:

  • Missed-call text-back: An automated text sent after a missed call, asking how the business can help
  • Conversational follow-up: AI-assisted text responses to help you describe your needs and schedule an appointment
  • Appointment confirmation: Text messages confirming booked appointments
  • Website chat follow-up: If you initiate a conversation via the website chat widget and provide your phone number, you may receive follow-up texts

2. Consent and Opt-In

By calling a business that uses SnagThatLead AI, you consent to receive a text message from that business if your call is not answered. This consent is based on your initiation of contact with the business. You are not required to consent to text messaging as a condition of purchasing any goods or services.

If you use a website chat widget powered by SnagThatLead and voluntarily provide your phone number, you consent to receive follow-up text messages related to your inquiry.

3. Opt-Out

You can opt out of receiving text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive. You will receive a single confirmation message acknowledging your opt-out, and no further messages will be sent.

You may also opt out by:

  • Replying STOP, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, or QUIT to any message
  • Emailing hello@snagthatlead.com with your phone number and a request to stop messages

After opting out, you will not receive any further text messages from that business through our platform unless you initiate contact again.
